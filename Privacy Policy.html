At Gold Menu, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what information our application uses and how it is handled.

What Data We Access

Gold Menu only checks the following:

  • If AutoHotkey is installed on your system.
  • If the macro files or configuration files created by Gold Menu exist.
  • Your subscriber verification code (linked to your Discord account) to confirm access to features.

What Data We Store

The only data stored is your subscriber verification code. No personal information, system details, or browsing data are collected.

How Your Data Is Used

The subscriber code is used solely to verify your eligibility to access Gold Menu features. This information is never sold, shared, or used for tracking purposes.

Security

Gold Menu is designed to keep your information safe. All checks and storage are limited to your own device.
